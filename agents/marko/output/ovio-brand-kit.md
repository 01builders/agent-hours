## 🎨 Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Horizon Blue | `#2A6EBB` | Main brand color. Used in the logo, primary CTAs, headers, and navigation. |
| **Secondary** | Uplift Green | `#3DBF8C` | Growth and positivity. Used in success states, highlights, the logo "O" monogram, and secondary buttons. |
| **Accent** | Sunrise Amber | `#F5A623` | Used sparingly for delight. CTAs, milestone celebrations, logo sunrise accent, key stat callouts. |
| **Neutral Dark** | Slate | `#2C3E50` | Body text, wordmark, UI chrome, and iconography. |
| **Neutral Light** | Cloud White | `#F4F7FB` | Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, logo center. Creates breathing room and calm. |

### Color Principles
- **Never use red** — it triggers alarm and loss aversion in a financial context
- Horizon Blue and Uplift Green are co-equal — neither dominates; they represent the journey from debt (Blue: steady, safe) to freedom (Green: growth, arrival)
- Sunrise Amber should never exceed 10–15% of any composition — it is a highlight, not a base
- All color combinations must meet **WCAG AA contrast standards** minimum for accessibility

## 🖋️ Typography

### Primary — Headlines & Display
**Plus Jakarta Sans Bold**
- Use for: hero headlines, section titles, logo wordmark, large UI labels
- Feel: modern, geometric confidence with human warmth
- Source: [Google Fonts — Plus Jakarta Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Plus+Jakarta+Sans)

### Secondary — Body & UI
**Inter Regular / Medium**
- Use for: body copy, form labels, fine print, data tables, navigation
- Feel: neutral, highly legible, the gold standard for financial UI
- Source: [Google Fonts — Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

### Type Scale (Recommended)
| Level | Font | Weight | Size |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hero | Plus Jakarta Sans | Bold | 48–64px |
| H1 | Plus Jakarta Sans | Bold | 36px |

### Typography Rules
- Never use more than 2 typefaces
- Minimum body text size: 14px
- Line height for body: 1.6x font size
- Never center-align body copy — left-aligned only for readability

## ✍️ Brand Voice & Tone

### Tone Adjectives
**Clear · Empowering · Steady · Human · Optimistic**

### Personality
Ovio speaks like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be a financial expert — not a bank, not a debt collector, not a salesperson. Warm but precise. Encouraging but honest. Never condescending, never alarmist.

### Voice Examples
| ✅ Do Say | ❌ Don't Say |
|---|---|
| *"You've got this. Let's make your money work smarter."* | *"Crush your debt NOW!"* |
| *"One simple rate. Zero confusion. Full control."* | *"Apply for financial relief today."* |
| *"We don't just lower your APR — we raise your peace of mind."* | *"Are you drowning in debt?"* |
| *"See exactly what you'll save, before you commit."* | *"Limited time offer — act fast!"* |
| *"Your path to zero starts here."* | *"Don't let debt ruin your life."* |

### Writing Principles
1. **Lead with outcomes** — *"Save $400/month"* not *"consolidate multiple accounts"*
2. **Use plain language** — explain APR, consolidation, and terms as if the reader is hearing them for the first time
3. **Be specific** — real numbers, real savings, real timelines build trust
4. **Celebrate progress** — acknowledge milestones, no matter how small
5. **Never use guilt** — debt is a circumstance, not a character flaw

## ✅ Brand Do's & Don'ts

### Do's
1. Use whitespace generously — clarity in design mirrors clarity in product
2. Show real numbers and transparent savings calculations
3. Use photography that shows aspiration — people looking forward, light, open spaces
4. Celebrate customer milestones visually and in copy
5. Keep UI flows simple — every extra step costs trust

### Don'ts
1. Never use red in any brand context — not even for error states (use a muted orange instead)
2. Avoid heavy gradients, complex textures, or flashy animations
3. Never use stock imagery of stressed, worried, or sad people
4. Don't bury fees, caveats, or APR ranges in fine print — transparency is the brand
5. Avoid financial jargon without immediate plain-language explanation

- Profile image: Icon-only logo on Horizon Blue `#2A6EBB` background
